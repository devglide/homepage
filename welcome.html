Welcome everyone to Version 3 of our site! I’m thrilled to unveil a complete overhaul, rebuilt from the ground up using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. One of my main goals in this redesign has been to enhance speed, particularly as our server operates within limited bandwidth constraints. I’ve streamlined the site's functionality to ensure quick access to content while maintaining a smooth user experience.
In Version 3, I’ve put the focus on two main pages: a journal documenting class activities and a video page where you can enjoy the various songs the kids love. Emphasizing anonymity, I’ve implemented measures to blur children's faces in images and while at the same time still capturing their creative works. This commitment to privacy ensures a safe and respectful environment for all visitors.
Looking ahead, future updates, marked by version 3.X increments, will continue to prioritize speed enhancements, backend code, refined readability, and improvements to page layout. I acknowledge that the current layout may not be perfect, as I’ve been learning and evolving my programming skills along the way. I would also like to extend a warm invitation to parents to explore my GitHub repository, where they can contribute to code improvements or offer insights to enhance page layout and functionality.
